Built for travel businesses

Built for Travel Agents, OTAs, DMCs and Tour Operators

RezLive.com is designed for professional travel partners who need reliable access to global travel inventory. Travel agents, travel agencies, online travel agencies, destination management companies and tour operators can use RezLive to search, compare and book travel services for leisure, business and group travellers.

Launched in 2007 in India, RezLive.com has grown from Ahmedabad into a global B2B travel technology brand with 250+ employees and 27+ offices worldwide.
